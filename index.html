Who am I?

Background

My name is Warren H. I was born in Rwanda in the family of 7 children. The country of Rwanda is in centre of Africa with a population of about 12 million with the area of 26 338 sqkm. The majority of the population is the youth. The native language in Rwanda is called Kinyarwanda and that is the one language spoken by all the population. Apart form that, at school people learn french and english. Currently, all those three languages are used officially. The average maximum temperature in Rwanda is 26 degree celcius while the minimum average temperature is 15 degree celcius.

Education

I studied my primary and secondary education in Rwanda.I finished my secondary education in 1996. I studied one year in National University of Rwanda before getting a scholarship and travel to India to study Civil Engineering. I completed my bachelor degree in 2002, and returned back home. I worked for one private company for about a year then joined the Unit of Schools Construction toward the end of the year 2003. I assumed different role in the unit of schools construction. In the year 2013 I moved to Hong Kong.

About job

He had the most of his job in Rwanda. He worked for the private construction company for about a year before joining the Schools Construction Unit of the Ministry of Education. He played a great role in increasing and improving the schools infrastructure in Rwanda, which made the 9-year and 12-year basic education possible. Just in the span of 3 years from 2009, his unit constructed more than 9 000 classrooms across the country. After that great achievement, the students enrolment rate in primary education greatly impoved and came closer to 100%.

His job included both technical knowledge in the construction as well as management skills. Apart from few staff in the central office, he had more than 500 people working for that project around the country. Among the staff under his supervision included engineers, logisticians, human resource, site techinicians. His job included management of contracts for supply of material and to ensure that contractors are paid on time.
